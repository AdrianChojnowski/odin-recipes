Peanut Butter Noodles

Description

These 10 minute peanut noodles are creamy, chewy, and delicious. This recipe only requires common Asian pantry ingredients that are not hard to find. My favorite noodles to use are udon noodles. And for those who don't like it spicy, just leave out the chili crisp.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 200g Udon Noodles
  • 1 tbsp. Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Peanut Butter
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 0.5 tbsp. roasted sesame seeds
  • 0.5 tbsp. chilli powder
  • 2 tbsp. pasta water
  • 3 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp. chopped green onion

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil Udon Noodles following the package instructions
  2. While the noodles are boiling grab a separate bowl
  3. To the bowl add:
    • 1 tbsp. Soy Sauce
    • 1 tsp Oyster sauce
    • 1 tbsp. Peanut Butter
    • 1 tsp garlic paste
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 0.5 tbsp. chilli powder
    • 0.5 tbsp. roasted sesame seeds
  4. Add 3 table spoons of vegetable oil to a pan and set the heat to medium-high
  5. Once the oil is hot fry green onions for 30 seconds
  6. After 30 seconds add the oil and green onions to the bowl with sauce
  7. Mix everything well and then add 2 tbsp. of pasta water
  8. Mix the sauce with noodles and serve
